# Project Overview

`LGD-commercial` is the commercial loss-given-default and recovery engine in the public credit-risk stack.

## Portfolio role

It converts borrower risk context, facility structure, collateral assumptions, and recovery logic into downturn LGD and recovery outputs for downstream loss, stress, pricing, and capital analysis.

## Upstream inputs

- `PD-and-scorecard-commercial`
- `industry-analysis`

## Downstream consumers

- `expected-loss-engine-commercial`
- `stress-testing-commercial`
- `RAROC-pricing-and-return-hurdle`
- `RWA-capital-commercial`
